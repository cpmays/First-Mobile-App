Numbers

Numbers in programming are gerenally handled in one of the follow ways based upon their values.

Basic Numbers

Basic numbers that the role of integers,(keyword being "int"), and their values can range from -32,768 to 32,767 or -2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647

Small Numbers

Smaller numbers that the role of shorts,(keyword being "short"), and their values can range from -32,768 to 32,767.

Longer Numbers

Long numbers that the role of longs,(keyword being "long"), and their values can range from -2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647

Decimal Numbers

Decimal numbers that the role of floats,(keyword being "float"), and their values can range from 1.2E-38 to 3.4E+38 till 6 decimal places
